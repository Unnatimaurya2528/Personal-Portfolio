Poems

Those Days

All the birds flew away,
Bowling as I watch 'em go
The empty nests the still domain, no I can't take it anymore.
The birds weren't pretty anyway, But atleast they came on the way
With their Wings, they would hide, All the dark bitter side.
Now the birds are long gone,
I am here all alone,
With no one beside me to meet,
On the path I put my feet.

Double Dice

I rolled the dice, Not once but twice.
The first was light,
It told me lies.
The second fell, But made no sound.
A door was there, No walls around.

Double Dice pt.2

I rolled the dice, Not once but twice. One kissed the flame, A whisper bled,
One slept in ice.
A silence spoke.
The game was rigged
Yet still, I woke.

Desires

Dangerous desires dance,
Draped in dusk,
Shadows' sinister song
Silences Trust...
Is it passion, or poison art? A dangerous thief of soul & heart.
